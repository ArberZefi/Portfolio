About me

I am an indie game developer awarded at the Hellenic game awards 2022, with a rich background of 3 years in Unity game development. Reflecting on my journey, I take pride in the achievements so far and aspire to embark on more substantial projects while extending my expertise to freelancing opportunities.

Outside the realm of game development, I have diverse interests. Whether it's setting new personal records in the gym or immersing myself in the tranquility of nature through camping, each experience fuels my creative inspiration for game design.

As a seasoned Unity game developer deeply immersed in the world of programming, my expertise lies in the dynamic realm of C#. Over the last three years, I've passionately refined my skills in game development, immersing myself in the craft through hands-on experience and active participation in events and game jams.

Proudly representing Konnios Games, my team recently clinched success by securing the prestigious Hellenic Game Awards 2023 for our standout creation, "Insect Dude." This accomplishment stands as a testament to our commitment to excellence and innovative game design.

Beyond accolades, my journey has seen the publication of two noteworthy games on the Play Store and several others on itch.io, showcasing a diverse range of projects that reflect my dedication to pushing creative boundaries. This commitment is not merely a professional pursuit but a genuine passion for crafting games that captivate and engage players.

Currently, my focus is directed towards exciting new projects, each a canvas for innovation and a testament to the evolving landscape of game development. With a proven track record of success and an unwavering commitment to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the gaming industry, I am eager to continue my journey, contributing to the creation of memorable and immersive gaming experiences.
